ME and my story with Climbing Hills 🗿

It's been 3 years since my school friends and I started to climb Dighi Hills on weekends and holidays. We started it in lockdown and still going on. In lockdown, we used to cover the distance of 8 km from Town to Hills on a cycle. But the thing is different from the past year... we all started using bikes to reach, and now none of us has our cycle in the right condition to ride it. It's been fun all the time, and the only part difficult was to wake up at 5 am and phone call everyone to gather together. My friend Swaraj would always keep us waiting in the cold morning and used to be late every time. While Dnyanesh is still doing the same today, always late!
